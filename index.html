Enterprise Sales Playbook
Discovery Call Framework
45-60 minutes
1

Context Setting (5 min)

Establish credibility, set agenda, confirm stakeholders.

"Thanks for making time. Before we dive in — who else should be part of this conversation as we explore whether there's a fit?"
2

Current State Discovery (15 min)

Map their data landscape and identify pain.

"Walk me through your current data stack. How many systems are you running? What happens when leadership asks for a cross-functional report?"
"How much time does your team spend pulling data together vs actually using it?"
3

Impact Quantification (10 min)

Attach dollars to the problem.

"If you had to estimate — what's the fully-loaded cost of the manual work we just discussed? Hours per week times people times rate?"
"What decisions aren't being made — or are being made slowly — because the data isn't there?"
4

Future State Vision (10 min)

Paint the picture of what's possible.

"What if you could ask any question about your business — across all your systems — and get an answer in seconds? What would that change?"
5

Next Steps (5 min)

"Based on what you've shared, I think a Pilot Program makes sense — 90 days, one use case, prove the value before a larger rollout. The investment is $25-50K depending on scope. Who else needs to be involved in that decision?"
Pilot to Enterprise Expansion
60 minutes
1

Pilot Results Review (15 min)

"Let's walk through what we delivered in the pilot. You had [X problem]. We built [Y solution]. The results: [Z outcome — time saved, decisions enabled, etc.]"
2

Expansion Opportunity (15 min)

"The pilot covered one use case. Based on our audit, there are 5 more high-impact opportunities across your organization. Here's what full deployment looks like..."
3

Investment + ROI (15 min)

"Full implementation is $[500K-2M] over [6-12 months]. Based on the pilot results, we project [X] in savings and [Y] in new revenue enabled. Payback period: [Z months]."
4

Procurement Process (10 min)

"What does your internal approval process look like for an investment of this size? Who needs to sign off? What can I provide to support that process?"
