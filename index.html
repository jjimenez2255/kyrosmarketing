Scorecard to Audit Conversion Call
30 minutes
1

Review Their Scorecard (5 min)

They already bought, so they're qualified. Start with value.

"I reviewed your Scorecard results. You scored [X] overall, but I noticed [specific weak area]. Let's dig into that."
2

Quantify the Pain (10 min)

Make them feel the cost of inaction.

"Your Scorecard flagged [issue]. How much time does your team spend on that each week?"
"What decisions are you not making because the data isn't there?"
3

Present the Audit (10 min)

Position as the logical next step.

"The Scorecard identified the gaps. The Audit maps exactly how to fix them. We inventory every system, map the relationships, and give you a blueprint."
4

Close (5 min)

"It's $20K and takes 2-3 weeks. Based on your Scorecard, I think we can save you [X hours/week]. Want to move forward?"
